<p><p>Food Grade Sodium Alginate is available in two main types in the market - powder and granular. Powdered sodium alginate is finely ground and easily dissolvable in water, making it suitable for applications such as thickening, gelling, and emulsifying in food products. On the other hand, granular sodium alginate is larger in particle size, providing a more controlled release of alginate in food formulations. Both types offer unique advantages and are widely used in the food industry for various purposes.</p></p>
