<p><p>Interventional cardiology market consists of various segments including cardiology catheters, guidewires, balloon catheters, coronary stents, fractional flow reserve, and intravascular ultrasound. Cardiology catheters are used for diagnosing and treating heart conditions, while guidewires help navigate through blood vessels during procedures. Balloon catheters are used to open narrowed arteries, and coronary stents are placed to keep them open. Fractional flow reserve measures blood flow and intravascular ultrasound provides detailed images of the inside of blood vessels. Each segment plays a crucial role in interventional cardiology procedures.</p></p>
