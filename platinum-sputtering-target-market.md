Rotary Type targets are designed with a rotating mechanism to ensure uniform erosion and longer target life. They are commonly used in large-scale industrial applications. On the other hand, Non Rotatable Type targets are stationary and are preferred for smaller-scale or laboratory settings. Both types offer high purity and consistency in sputtering processes, catering to different market needs based on their specific requirements.</p></p>
