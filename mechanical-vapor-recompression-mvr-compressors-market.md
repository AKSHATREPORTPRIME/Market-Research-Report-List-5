<p><p>Mechanical Vapor Recompression (MVR) compressors are used in processes where vapor needs to be compressed to a higher temperature. There are two main types of MVR compressors: Centrifugal and Roots MVR compressors. Centrifugal MVR compressors use rotating impellers to increase the vapor pressure, while Roots MVR compressors use two rotating lobes to compress the vapor. Both types of compressors are commonly used in industries such as chemical, pharmaceutical, and food processing for their energy efficiency and ability to recover heat energy.</p></p>
