<p><p>Next Generation Sequencing (NGS) market includes different types such as Targeted Sequencing, Whole Exome Sequencing, and Whole Genome Sequencing. Targeted Sequencing focuses on specific regions of the genome, providing detailed information on genes of interest. Whole Exome Sequencing examines all protein-coding regions of the genome, offering insights into potential disease-causing mutations. Whole Genome Sequencing analyzes the entire DNA sequence, uncovering variations across the entire genome. Each market type caters to different research and clinical applications, driving innovation in personalized medicine and genetic testing.</p></p>
