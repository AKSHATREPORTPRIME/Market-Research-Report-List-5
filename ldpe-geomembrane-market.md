<p><p>LDPE Geomembrane is a type of liner used in various industries for containment purposes. There are three main types of LDPE Geomembrane manufacturing processes: Extrusion, Calendering, and Blown Film. Extrusion involves melting LDPE resin and shaping it into a thin film. Calendering uses heavy rollers to flatten and shape the LDPE into a thin sheet. Blown Film process involves blowing melted LDPE through a circular die to create a thin film. Each process has its own advantages and is used based on specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
