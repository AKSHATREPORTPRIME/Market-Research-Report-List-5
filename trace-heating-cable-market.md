<p><p>Trace heating cables are used to prevent pipes and tanks from freezing in cold weather. There are four main types of trace heating cables: self-regulating heating cable, constant wattage heating cable, mineral insulated heating cable, and skin-effect heating cable. Self-regulating cables adjust their heat output based on the surrounding temperature, constant wattage cables provide a consistent amount of heat, mineral insulated cables are durable and suitable for high-temperature applications, and skin-effect cables are used for long-distance pipe heating. Each type has its own unique characteristics and applications.</p></p>
