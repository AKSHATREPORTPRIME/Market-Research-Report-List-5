<p><p>Free Space Optics (FSO) market includes modulators, transmitters, demodulators, receivers, encoders, and decoders. Modulators are used to encode data onto a light beam, transmitters send this beam through free space, demodulators decode the data from the received beam, receivers capture the beam and extract the data, encoders convert digital data to optical signals, and decoders do the opposite. On the other hand, Visible Light Communication (VLC) uses light for data transmission, enabling communication through light sources like LEDs.</p></p>
