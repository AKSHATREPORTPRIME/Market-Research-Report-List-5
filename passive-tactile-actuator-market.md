<p><p>Passive tactile actuators are categorized into three main types in the market: Eccentric Rotating Mass (ERM) Actuators, Linear Resonant Actuators (LRA), and Others. ERM actuators use an unbalanced motor to generate vibrations, LRA actuators use a mass attached to a spring to create vibrations, and other types of actuators include piezoelectric actuators and haptic feedback devices. Each type offers unique features and benefits to cater to different applications in the consumer electronics, automotive, and healthcare industries.</p></p>
