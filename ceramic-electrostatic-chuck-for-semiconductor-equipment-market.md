<p><strong>The Ceramic Electrostatic Chuck for Semiconductor Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coulomb Type</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Coulomb Type Ceramic Electrostatic Chuck is designed with a simple electrode configuration which utilizes the Coulomb force for holding semiconductor wafers in place during processing. On the other hand, Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type Ceramic Electrostatic Chuck uses a more complex electrode design which incorporates a combination of Coulomb and Johnsen-Rahbek forces to achieve better wafer gripping performance. Both types of ceramic electrostatic chucks are widely used in semiconductor equipment for their ability to securely hold wafers without causing damage.</p></p>
