<p><p>The Airborne Photoelectric Targeting System market includes two main types: multispectral and hyperspectral. Multispectral systems utilize multiple wavelengths in the visible and near-infrared spectrum to capture images and identify targets. Hyperspectral systems, on the other hand, use hundreds of narrow bands across the electromagnetic spectrum to provide highly detailed information about the composition of targets. Both types of systems are used in military applications for reconnaissance, surveillance, and target identification.</p></p>
