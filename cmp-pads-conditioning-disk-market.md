<p><p>Conventional CMP Pads Conditioners are widely used in the semiconductor industry for polishing wafers. They are made of materials such as polyurethane and are designed to effectively condition the surface of CMP pads. On the other hand, CVD Diamond Pad Conditioners use chemical vapor deposition (CVD) technology to deposit diamond particles onto the conditioning disk, offering superior hardness and durability for more aggressive pad conditioning. Both types of pad conditioners play a crucial role in maintaining the effectiveness and longevity of CMP pads in semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
