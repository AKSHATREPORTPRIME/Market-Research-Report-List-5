<p><strong>The Fridge Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Granular Activated Carbon (GAC) Filters</li><li>Carbon Block Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Granular Activated Carbon (GAC) Filters are commonly used in refrigerators to remove impurities and bad odors from water and ice. These filters contain small carbon particles that attract and absorb contaminants. Carbon Block Filters, on the other hand, are made of compressed carbon blocks that also remove impurities but have a higher filtration efficiency. They are preferred for their ability to remove smaller particles and provide cleaner, better-tasting water. Both types of filters are popular choices for refrigerator water filtration systems.</p></p>
