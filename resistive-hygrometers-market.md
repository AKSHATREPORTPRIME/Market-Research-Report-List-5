<p><p>Resistive hygrometers can be classified into two main types: relative type and absolute type. The relative type measures humidity levels relative to the ambient temperature, making it suitable for general use. On the other hand, the absolute type provides a more accurate reading by directly measuring the absolute humidity level, making it ideal for more precise applications. Both types cater to different needs and offer varying levels of accuracy in humidity measurement.</p></p>
