<p><strong>The Metagenomic Sequencing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shotgun Metagenomic Sequencing</li><li>16S rRNA Sequencing</li><li>Metatranscriptomics</li><li>Whole-genome Sequencing & De Novo Assembly</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metagenomic Sequencing Market involves various types such as Shotgun Metagenomic Sequencing, which analyzes all genetic material in a sample. 16S rRNA Sequencing specifically targets the bacterial 16S rRNA gene for bacterial community analysis. Metatranscriptomics focuses on the RNA transcripts present in a sample to understand gene expression. Whole-genome Sequencing looks at the entire genetic makeup of an organism. De Novo Assembly is used to reconstruct genomes from short read sequences without a reference genome. Each type offers unique insights into microbial communities and genetic information.</p></p>
