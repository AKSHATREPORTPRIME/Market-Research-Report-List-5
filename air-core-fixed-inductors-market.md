<p><p>Air core fixed inductors are categorized into two main types: multi-layer type and wire wound type. Multi-layer type inductors are made by stacking multiple layers of coil windings, allowing for high inductance values in a compact size. Wire wound type inductors are constructed using a single wire wound around a core, offering higher current-handling capacity and lower resistance. Both types are widely used in various electronic circuits for signal filtering, frequency tuning, and impedance matching applications.</p></p>
