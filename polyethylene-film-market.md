<p><p>Polyethylene film market can be divided into two main types based on density: High Density Polyethylene (HDPE) and Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE) films. HDPE films are known for their high strength and stiffness, making them ideal for applications requiring durability and resistance to tearing. On the other hand, LDPE films are more flexible and have a higher impact strength, making them suitable for applications where stretchability and conformability are important. Both types of films have their own unique properties and cater to different market needs.</p></p>
