<p><p>Parallel robots are divided into two main market types based on their axis configuration: Multi-Axis and Two-Axis. Two-Axis parallel robots, on the other hand, have only two axes of movement, limiting their range of motion but offering simpler and more cost-effective solutions for specific tasks such as pick-and-place operations.
