<p><ul><li>Open Type Paint Booths</li><li>Closed Type Paint Booths</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Open type paint booths and closed type paint booths are two main types of paint booths available in the market. Open type paint booths are also known as open face booths, where the air flows from the front of the booth to the back. These booths are cost-effective and are often used for smaller projects. Closed type paint booths, on the other hand, are fully enclosed booths with controlled airflow. They are ideal for larger projects and provide better containment of overspray and contaminants.</p></p>
