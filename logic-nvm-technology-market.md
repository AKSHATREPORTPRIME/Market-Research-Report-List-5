E2PROM offers non-volatile memory storage with electrical programmability. OTP/MTP provides one-time or multiple-time programmable memory solutions. The Others category includes various emerging NVM technologies such as Ferroelectric RAM and Resistive RAM. Each market type serves different needs in the industry, catering to a wide range of applications.</p></p>
