<p><strong>The Conductive Graphene Filament Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Asphalt Based</li><li>Pan Based</li><li>Viscose Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Conductive graphene filament is classified into three types based on their materials: Asphalt Based, Pan Based, and Viscose Based. Asphalt Based filaments are derived from asphalt and are known for their high electrical conductivity. Pan Based filaments are made from polyacrylonitrile and are widely used for their mechanical strength. Viscose Based filaments are produced from viscose rayon and offer a more flexible and lightweight option. Each type of conductive graphene filament caters to different needs and applications in the market.</p></p>
