<p><p>The Oil & Gas Subsea Umbilicals, Risers, and Flowlines market consists of three main components: umbilicals, risers, and flowlines. Umbilicals are cables used to transfer power, control, and chemicals to subsea equipment. Risers are pipes that connect subsea wells to surface platforms, allowing for the transportation of oil and gas. Flowlines are pipelines that transport oil and gas from subsea wells to processing facilities onshore. Each component plays a crucial role in the efficient operation of subsea oil and gas production systems.</p></p>
