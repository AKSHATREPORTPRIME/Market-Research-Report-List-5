<p><p>Soil Permeability Apparatus is used to measure the flow of water through soil samples. There are two main types of Soil Permeability Apparatus in the market: Constant Head and Falling Head. </p><p>Constant Head Soil Permeability Apparatus maintains a constant water level on one side of the soil sample to measure the flow rate. Falling Head Soil Permeability Apparatus measures the time it takes for water to flow through the soil under gravity, using a falling water column. Both types are essential for studying the permeability of different types of soil.</p></p>
