<p><p>Surgical laser scalpels come in various types, with the most common ones being CO2 lasers and Argon lasers. CO2 lasers are widely used for cutting, vaporizing, and cauterizing tissues due to their precision and minimal tissue damage. Argon lasers are preferred for their ability to coagulate blood vessels effectively. There are also other types of surgical laser scalpels on the market, each with its own unique capabilities and applications, such as Diode lasers, Nd:YAG lasers, and Excimer lasers. Each type has specific advantages and is chosen based on the surgical procedure being performed.</p></p>
