<p><p>Scroll saw market can be divided into two main types: C-arm and parallel arm designs. </p><p>The C-arm scroll saw has a distinctive shape resembling the letter "C", with the blade mounted between the two arms. This design allows for better visibility of the cutting line and easier repositioning of the workpiece.</p><p>On the other hand, the parallel arm scroll saw features two parallel arms connected by a rod, with the blade mounted on one arm. This design offers stability and precision in cutting intricate patterns.</p><p>Both types cater to different preferences and needs of woodworkers and craftsmen.</p></p>
