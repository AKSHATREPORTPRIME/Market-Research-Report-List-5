<p><p>Tray lidding films come in various types based on the material used. Polyethylene (PE) films are known for their flexibility and seal strength. Polypropylene (PP) films offer excellent clarity and barrier properties. Ethylene Vinyl Alcohol (EVOH) films provide superior oxygen barrier properties. Polyethylene terephthalate (PET) films are ideal for high-temperature applications. Other types of tray lidding films include polystyrene films, which offer good impact resistance, and aluminum foil films, which provide excellent barrier properties against moisture and light. Each type of film offers unique properties to suit different packaging needs.</p></p>
