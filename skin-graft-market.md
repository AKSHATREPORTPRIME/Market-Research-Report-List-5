<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Skin Graft Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Autologous</li><li>Isogeneic</li><li>Allogeneic</li><li>Xenogeneic</li><li>Prosthetic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Skin grafts are used to treat skin wounds and burns. Autologous grafts involve using the patient's own skin, which reduces the risk of rejection. Isogeneic grafts use skin from an identical twin, while allogeneic grafts use skin from a non-related donor. Xenogeneic grafts use skin from a different species, such as pigs. Prosthetic grafts involve using synthetic materials. Each type of skin graft has its own advantages and limitations, which are chosen based on the individual patient's needs and circumstances.</p></p>
