C-Type conveyors have a continuous Z-shape layout, ideal for handling fragile or delicate items. E-Type conveyors are equipped with accumulating or indexing capabilities, making them suitable for high-speed and precise material handling. Each type caters to specific industry needs, offering versatility and efficiency in vertical transportation of goods.</p></p>
