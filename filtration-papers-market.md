<p><p>Filtration papers are divided into two main types based on their purpose: Qualitative Filtration Papers and Quantitative Filtration Papers. Qualitative papers are used for general filtration where the exact measurement of particles is not critical, while Quantitative papers are designed for precise measurement of particles. Qualitative papers are commonly used in routine laboratory applications, whereas Quantitative papers are preferred for tasks that require accurate determination of particulate matter. Both types play a crucial role in various industries for separating solids from liquids or gases.</p></p>
