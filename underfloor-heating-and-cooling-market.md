<p><p>Underfloor Heating and Cooling systems are divided into two main types: Electric System and Hydronic System. Electric systems use electric cables or mats to heat the floor, while hydronic systems use water-filled tubes. The Electric System market is popular for its easier installation and individual room control options, while the Hydronic System market is known for its energy efficiency and even distribution of heat. Both types offer a comfortable and efficient way to heat or cool a space from below the floor.</p></p>
