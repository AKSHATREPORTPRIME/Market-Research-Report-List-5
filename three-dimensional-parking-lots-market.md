Simple lift systems involve a single vertical movement. Roadway stacking utilizes a system where vehicles are stacked on top of each other on roadways. Plane movement involves vehicles moving on a plane in various directions. Vertical lift systems move vehicles vertically within a structure. Vertical loop market types feature structures where vehicles move in a loop motion within the parking lot.</p></p>
