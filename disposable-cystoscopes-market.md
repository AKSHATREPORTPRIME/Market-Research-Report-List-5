<p><ul><li>Rigid Cystoscopes</li><li>Flexible Cystoscopes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Disposable cystoscopes are available in two main types - rigid cystoscopes and flexible cystoscopes. Rigid cystoscopes have a straight, inflexible shaft that allows for easy maneuvering and precise visualization of the bladder. On the other hand, flexible cystoscopes have a flexible shaft that allows for better navigation through the urethra and reaching more complex areas of the bladder. Both types of disposable cystoscopes offer advantages in terms of convenience, cost-effectiveness, and reduced risk of cross-contamination.</p></p>
