<p><ul><li>Conventional Parting Wax</li><li>Semi-Permanent Parting Wax</li><li>Silicone-Free Parting Wax</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Parting waxes can be divided into three main market types: Conventional, Semi-Permanent, and Silicone-Free. Conventional parting waxes are typically used for one-time applications and are easy to break apart. Semi-Permanent parting waxes offer a longer-lasting solution for repeated use, making them more cost-effective in the long run. Silicone-Free parting waxes are preferred in industries where silicone contamination is a concern, such as in the production of certain plastics or rubber products. Each type of parting wax caters to different market needs and preferences.</p></p>
