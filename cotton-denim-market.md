<p><p>Cotton denim is categorized into three main types based on their weight: light denim, medium denim, and heavy denim. Light denim typically ranges from 6-9 ounces per square yard and is ideal for lightweight and breathable clothing. Medium denim falls between 10-12 ounces and is commonly used for everyday jeans. Heavy denim, with a weight of 13 ounces or more, is known for its durability and is often used for workwear and jackets. Each market caters to different preferences and needs in the fashion industry.</p></p>
