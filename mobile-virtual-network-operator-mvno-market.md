<p><p>Mobile Virtual Network Operator (MVNO) market consists of three types, namely Reseller, Service Operator, and Full MVNO. Resellers purchase network services from MNOs and sell them to customers under their brand. Service Operators manage their own customer base and provide services using MNO infrastructure. Full MVNOs have complete control over their network infrastructure, including switching equipment and customer databases. Each type offers different levels of customization and control over services, catering to varying business models and market segments within the mobile industry.</p></p>
