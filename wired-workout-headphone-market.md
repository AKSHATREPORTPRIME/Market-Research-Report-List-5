<p><p>The Wired Workout Headphone market can be categorized into two types: Back Closed-back Type and Back Open-back Type. </p><p>Back Closed-back Type headphones have a sealed ear cup design which ensures that sound is contained within the ear cup. This type of headphone is ideal for workout environments where noise isolation is important. </p><p>On the other hand, Back Open-back Type headphones have an open ear cup design which allows airflow and sound to escape, resulting in a more natural sound quality. These headphones are suitable for users who prefer a more realistic and open sound experience during workouts.</p></p>
