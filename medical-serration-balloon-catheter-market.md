It is typically used in procedures where the catheter needs to remain in place for an extended period of time. Rapid Exchange Serration Balloon Catheter, on the other hand, features a shorter shaft and is designed for easier and quicker exchange during procedures. Both types of catheters are commonly used in the treatment of coronary artery disease and other vascular conditions.</p></p>
