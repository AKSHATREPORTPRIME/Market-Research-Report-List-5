<p><p>Polyurethane waterproof paint is available in two main types - Crystalline and Water Emulsion. Crystalline waterproof paint forms a crystalline structure when applied, creating a barrier against water penetration. On the other hand, Water Emulsion waterproof paint is water-based and forms a flexible film on the surface, providing protection against water damage. Both types of waterproof paint are commonly used in construction and building applications to prevent leaks and damage caused by water infiltration.</p></p>
