<p><p>Hydrostatic input liquid level transmitters come in two main types: Rod Level Transmitters and Cable Level Transmitters. Rod level transmitters use a metal rod immersed in the liquid to measure the level, while cable level transmitters use a cable and pulley system. Both types offer accurate and reliable measurements of liquid levels in tanks and vessels. These transmitters are widely used in industries such as oil and gas, chemical processing, and water treatment to monitor and control fluid levels.</p></p>
