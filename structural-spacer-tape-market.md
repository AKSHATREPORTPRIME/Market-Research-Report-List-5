<p><p>Structural spacer tape is available in two main market types: double sided and single sided. Double sided spacer tape is designed to provide bonding on both sides, offering strong adhesion for structural applications. On the other hand, single sided spacer tape is only adhesive on one side, allowing for temporary fixation or protection during assembly. Both types of spacer tape are commonly used in construction, automotive, and electronic industries for bonding, insulation, and cushioning purposes.</p></p>
