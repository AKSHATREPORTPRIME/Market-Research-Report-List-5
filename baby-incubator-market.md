These incubators are lightweight, compact, and equipped with features such as battery backup and shock resistance. Normal baby incubators, on the other hand, are stationary units used in hospitals for premature or sick infants. They are larger in size and offer more advanced monitoring and temperature control capabilities. Both types play a crucial role in providing a controlled environment for newborns in need of intensive care.</p></p>
