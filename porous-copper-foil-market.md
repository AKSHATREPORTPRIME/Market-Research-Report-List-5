<p><ul><li>Porosity: Under 50μm</li><li>Porosity: 50μm - 100μm</li><li>Porosity: Above 100μm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Porous copper foil is classified into three types based on porosity levels. Porosity under 50μm, porosity 50μm - 100μm, and porosity above 100μm. Each type offers varying degrees of permeability and allows for different levels of air or fluid flow. Porous copper foil with porosity under 50μm provides a high level of filtration and thermal conductivity. Porosity between 50μm - 100μm offers a balanced mix of filtration and flexibility. Porosity above 100μm provides superior fluid flow and absorption capabilities.</p></p>
