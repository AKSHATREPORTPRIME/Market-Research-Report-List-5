<p><ul><li>External Gear Pump</li><li>Internal Gear Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gear pumps are widely used in liquid delivery equipment for various industrial applications. There are two main types of gear pumps - external gear pump and internal gear pump. External gear pumps consist of two gears rotating externally in a close-fitting housing, while internal gear pumps have a smaller gear inside a larger gear. External gear pumps are commonly used for high-pressure applications, while internal gear pumps are preferred for high-viscosity fluids. Both types offer efficient and reliable liquid delivery performance in different scenarios.</p></p>
