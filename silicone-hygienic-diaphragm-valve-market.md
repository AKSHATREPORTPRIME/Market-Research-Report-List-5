<p><p>Silicone hygienic diaphragm valves are categorized into two main types - Weir Type and Weirless Radial Diaphragm Type. Weir Type valves have a weir or raised edge around the valve seat, which provides a better sealing surface for the diaphragm. Weirless Radial Diaphragm Type valves, on the other hand, do not have a weir, allowing for easier cleaning and maintenance. Both types are commonly used in hygienic applications in industries such as food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and biotechnology.</p></p>
