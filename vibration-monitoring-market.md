<p><p>Vibration monitoring market consists of two main types: Online vibration monitoring and offline vibration monitoring. Online vibration monitoring involves real-time monitoring of vibration levels in machines and equipment using sensors and wireless technology. This allows for immediate detection of any abnormalities or potential issues. Offline vibration monitoring, on the other hand, involves periodic manual measurements of vibration levels using handheld devices. Both types play a crucial role in predictive maintenance strategies to ensure smooth operations and prevent equipment failure.</p></p>
