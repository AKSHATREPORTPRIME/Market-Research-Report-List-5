<p><p>The mountain bike bicycle suspension market consists of two main types: hardtail suspension and full suspension. Hardtail suspension bikes have a suspension fork in the front to absorb shocks from the terrain, while the rear remains rigid. On the other hand, full suspension bikes have suspension both in the front and rear, providing a smoother ride by absorbing shocks from all directions. Both types cater to different preferences and terrain types, offering varying levels of comfort, control, and traction for mountain biking enthusiasts.</p></p>
