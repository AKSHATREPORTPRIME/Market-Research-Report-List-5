<p><p>Automatic honing machines are available in two main types: vertical and horizontal. Vertical honing machines are designed to hone the inner surface of cylindrical workpieces by moving the workpiece up and down in a controlled vertical motion. Horizontal honing machines, on the other hand, are designed to hone the inner surface of flat or round workpieces by moving the workpiece back and forth in a horizontal motion. Both types of machines are used in various industries for precision surface finishing applications.</p></p>
