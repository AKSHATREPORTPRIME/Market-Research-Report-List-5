<p><p>Payment card personalization solutions refer to techniques used to customize payment cards with unique designs, information, and security features. The market types include Dye Sublimation, Retransfer Printing, and Inkjet Printing. </p><p>Dye Sublimation technology involves printing the design onto a special transfer paper which is then transferred onto the card using heat. Retransfer Printing uses a two-step process where the design is first printed onto a retransfer film then transferred onto the card surface. Inkjet Printing directly applies the design onto the card surface using ink droplets.</p></p>
