Oil-soluble Pigments, on the other hand, are pigments that dissolve in oil and are used in oil-based cosmetics like lip balms and cream blushes. Both types of pigments play a crucial role in providing color and coverage in cosmetic products, catering to different formulation needs.</p></p>
