<p><p>The Airport Roller Deck Market is divided into two main types: Belt Connected and Chain Connected systems. Belt Connected roller decks use a continuous looped belt to transport luggage or cargo, providing a smooth and reliable operation. On the other hand, Chain Connected roller decks utilize a chain-driven system to move items along the deck, offering durability and strength for heavy-duty applications. Both types of roller decks are commonly used in airports for efficient and automated handling of baggage and cargo.</p></p>
