<p><p>Genetically Modified Organisms (GMO) Testing Market involves two main types of technologies: Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) Technology and Immunoassay Technology. PCR Technology is based on amplifying and detecting specific DNA sequences in a sample, making it highly sensitive and accurate for identifying GMOs. On the other hand, Immunoassay Technology relies on antibodies to detect GMO proteins, offering a quick and cost-effective way to screen for GMOs. Both technologies play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and regulation of GMO products in the market.</p></p>
