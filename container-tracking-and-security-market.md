<p><p>Container tracking and security market includes various technologies such as Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) and Real Time Locating Systems (RTLS) to monitor and manage the movement of containers. RFID uses radio waves to automatically identify and track tags attached to containers, allowing for real-time tracking and visibility. RTLS, on the other hand, uses technologies like GPS and Wi-Fi to provide accurate location information of containers. Both technologies are crucial in ensuring the security and efficiency of container logistics operations.</p></p>
