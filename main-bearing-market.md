<p><p>Main bearings are essential components in machinery and equipment, with two main types being sliding bearings and rolling bearings. Sliding bearings rely on a sliding motion between two surfaces to support the load, while rolling bearings use rolling elements, such as balls or rollers, to reduce friction and support the load. Sliding bearings are commonly used in applications where heavy loads and low speeds are present, while rolling bearings are more suitable for high-speed applications requiring low friction and high precision.</p></p>
