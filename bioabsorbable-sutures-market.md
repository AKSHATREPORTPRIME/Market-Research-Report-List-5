<p><strong>The Bioabsorbable Sutures Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Catgut</li><li>Polymer Chemical Synthesis Line</li><li>Pure Natural Collagen Suture</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bioabsorbable sutures are classified into different types based on their composition and source. Catgut sutures are made from natural animal collagen, specifically from sheep or goat intestines. Polymer chemical synthesis line sutures are produced synthetically using biodegradable polymers. Pure natural collagen sutures are derived from collagen extracted from bovine or equine sources. Other types of bioabsorbable sutures may include those made from plant-based materials or other natural proteins. Each type of suture offers unique characteristics and benefits for different surgical applications.</p></p>
