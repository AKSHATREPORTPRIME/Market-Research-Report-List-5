<p><p>Infusion tube heaters come in two main types: constant temperature and variable temperature. Constant temperature infusion tube heaters maintain a steady temperature for the duration of the procedure, ensuring consistent heat delivery to the infusion tube. On the other hand, variable temperature infusion tube heaters allow for temperature adjustments according to specific requirements, offering more flexibility in controlling the heat during the procedure. Both types cater to different needs and preferences, providing healthcare professionals with options to choose from based on their specific requirements.</p></p>
