<p><p>A Ductless Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning (HVAC) system, also known as mini-split or multi-split system, is a heating and cooling system that does not require ductwork to operate. Instead, it uses an outdoor condenser unit and one or more indoor air handling units to provide targeted heating or cooling to specific rooms or zones within a building. This system is popular in residential and commercial settings where traditional HVAC systems are not viable or cost-effective.</p><p>The Ductless HVAC system market is expected to grow at a Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR) of 7.8% during the forecast period.
