<p><ul><li>Conventional</li><li>Electric Side Mount</li><li>Gas Driven Side Mount</li><li>The Mini Unit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Horizontal Surface Pumps are classified into different types based on their design and functionality. Conventional pumps are the most common type, utilizing a standard configuration for pumping fluids. Electric Side Mount pumps are equipped with an electric motor positioned on the side for easy access and maintenance. Gas Driven Side Mount pumps rely on gas power for operation, making them suitable for remote areas without electricity. The Mini Unit market refers to smaller and more compact surface pumps, ideal for applications where space is limited or portability is required.</p></p>
