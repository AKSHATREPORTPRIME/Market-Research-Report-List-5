SF6 Circuit Breakers use sulfur hexafluoride gas for arc extinguishing, ideal for high voltage systems due to their compact design. Oil Circuit Breakers rely on oil for arc extinction, commonly used in medium voltage applications. Each type offers unique advantages and is used based on voltage requirements and system design.</p></p>
