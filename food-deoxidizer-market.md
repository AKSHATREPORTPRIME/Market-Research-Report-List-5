<p><ul><li>Metallic Deoxidizer</li><li>Non-metallic Deoxidizer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metallic deoxidizers are primarily made of metals such as iron, copper, or aluminum and work by removing oxygen from the surrounding environment through a chemical reaction. Non-metallic deoxidizers, on the other hand, are made of materials like silica gel or activated charcoal and absorb oxygen without undergoing a chemical reaction. Both types of deoxidizers are used in packaging to extend the shelf life of food products by reducing oxidation and spoilage, but they vary in their composition and mechanism of action.</p></p>
