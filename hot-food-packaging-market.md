<p><p>Hot food packaging market is divided into two main types, flexible packaging and rigid packaging. Flexible packaging is best suited for items that require a barrier against air and moisture, such as soups and sauces. It is lightweight, economical, and easily customizable. Rigid packaging, on the other hand, is ideal for products that need extra protection and stability, like pre-cooked meals. It is durable, stackable, and provides a higher level of protection during transportation and storage. Both types cater to different needs in the hot food packaging industry.</p></p>
