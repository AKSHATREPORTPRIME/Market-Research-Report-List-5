<p><p>Ambulatory EHR systems can be categorized into on-premise and cloud-based markets. On-premise systems require the software to be installed and maintained on the healthcare provider's own servers. This gives the providers full control over their data but can be costly and require IT expertise. Cloud-based systems, on the other hand, store data on remote servers accessed through the internet. This allows for easier scalability, lower upfront costs, and less need for IT maintenance, making it a popular choice among smaller practices and organizations.</p></p>
