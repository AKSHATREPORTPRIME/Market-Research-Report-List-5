<p><p>Thermal imaging technology is categorized into two main types: un-cooled thermal imaging and cryogenically cooled thermal imaging. Un-cooled thermal imaging systems are more cost-effective and do not require cooling elements, making them suitable for commercial applications. On the other hand, cryogenically cooled thermal imaging systems offer higher sensitivity and resolution due to their cooling process, making them ideal for military and scientific applications where precise detection is crucial. Both types play a significant role in various industries such as security, surveillance, firefighting, and medical imaging.</p></p>
