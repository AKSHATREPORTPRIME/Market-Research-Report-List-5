<p><ul><li>Wafer Probe Station</li><li>Die Bonder</li><li>Dicing Machine</li><li>Test handler</li><li>Sorter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor Packaging and Test Equipment market comprises various types of equipment such as Wafer Probe Stations, Die Bonders, Dicing Machines, Test Handlers, and Sorters. Wafer Probe Stations are used to test individual semiconductor devices on a wafer. Die Bonders are used to connect semiconductor dies to packages. Dicing Machines are used to cut semiconductor wafers into individual dies. Test Handlers are used to perform automated testing of packaged semiconductor devices. Sorters are used to classify and separate semiconductor devices based on their test results.</p></p>
