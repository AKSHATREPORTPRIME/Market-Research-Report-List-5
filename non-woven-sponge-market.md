<p><p>Non-woven sponges are available in two main types: sterile and non-sterile. Sterile non-woven sponges are individually wrapped to maintain their cleanliness and prevent contamination, making them suitable for use in medical settings and procedures where infection control is essential. On the other hand, non-sterile non-woven sponges are not individually packaged and are typically used for general cleaning, wound care, and other applications where sterility is not a primary concern. Both types of non-woven sponges offer absorbency and versatility for various uses.</p></p>
