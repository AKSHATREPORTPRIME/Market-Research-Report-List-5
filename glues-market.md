<p><p>There are three main types of glues in the market: water-based, solvent-based, and others. Water-based glues are environmentally friendly, easy to clean up, and have low odor. Solvent-based glues are known for their strong bonding properties and fast curing times, but can have a strong odor and release harmful volatile organic compounds. The "Others" market includes specialty glues such as hot glue, epoxy, and UV-cured adhesives, each with its own unique properties and applications. Each type of glue serves different purposes and is suited for specific projects.</p></p>
