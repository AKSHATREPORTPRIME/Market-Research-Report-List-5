<p><p>Fire Alarm Control Panel (FACP) is available in two main types - Conventional and Addressable. Conventional FACP is a basic system where detectors and devices are divided into zones, making it easier to identify the general location of a fire. Addressable FACP allows for each individual device to have its own unique address, providing more detailed information on the exact location of a fire. Both types are important in the fire alarm market, with addressable systems being more advanced and offering greater functionality.</p></p>
