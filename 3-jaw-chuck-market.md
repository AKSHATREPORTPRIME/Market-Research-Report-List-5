<p><ul><li>Front Mount</li><li>Rear Mount</li><li>Both Front and Rear</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Front mount 3 jaw chucks are mounted on the spindle nose and are commonly used for lightweight and small diameter workpieces. Rear mount 3 jaw chucks are mounted on the spindle bore and are preferred for heavier and larger diameter workpieces. Chucks that can be mounted on both the front and rear are versatile and allow for more flexibility in the type of workpieces that can be accommodated. Each type of chuck caters to specific machining requirements and offers distinct advantages in terms of precision and performance.</p></p>
