<p><p>Stationary dehumidifiers are available in different types to suit various needs. The conventional dehumidifier is a standalone unit that can be placed in any room to remove excess moisture from the air. On the other hand, air duct type dehumidifiers are integrated into the HVAC system and dehumidify the entire house through the ductwork. Both types are effective in controlling humidity levels and improving indoor air quality, but air duct type dehumidifiers offer a more comprehensive solution for whole-house dehumidification.</p></p>
