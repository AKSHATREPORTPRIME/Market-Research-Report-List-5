<p><p>The Exhaust Gas Cleaning System (EGCS) market includes three types: Open Loop, Closed Loop, and Mixed Waste Gas Purification Systems. Open Loop systems use seawater to clean exhaust gases, discharging the treated water back into the ocean. Closed Loop systems recirculate the wash water, treating it before discharge. Mixed Waste Gas Purification Systems combine both open and closed loop technologies to treat exhaust gases. Each type offers unique advantages and is tailored to meet specific environmental regulations and operational needs in the maritime industry.</p></p>
