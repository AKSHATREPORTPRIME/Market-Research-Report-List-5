<p><p>Medical Device Additive Manufacturing Market utilizes various technologies like Laser Sintering, Stereolithography, Electron Beam Melting, and Extrusion. Laser Sintering involves using a laser to bind powdered material together layer by layer. Stereolithography uses a laser to solidify liquid resin layer by layer. Electron Beam Melting uses an electron beam to melt and fuse metal powder. Extrusion involves squeezing material through a nozzle to build up layers. Each technology offers unique benefits and applications in the medical device industry.</p></p>
