<p><p>Dual-fuel engines are engines that can operate on a combination of diesel and gas. There are two main types of dual-fuel engines: four-stroke and two-stroke engines. Four-stroke dual-fuel engines have an intake stroke, compression stroke, power stroke, and exhaust stroke, making them more efficient. Two-stroke dual-fuel engines have a simpler design with fewer moving parts, making them lighter and more compact.
