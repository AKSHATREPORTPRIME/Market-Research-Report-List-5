<p><ul><li>Polyester-based TPU</li><li>Polyether-based TPU</li><li>Polycaprolactone-based TPU</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermoplastic Polyurethanes (TPU) are widely used in the automotive industry for various applications due to their excellent physical properties. There are three main types of TPU used in the automotive market: Polyester-based TPU, Polyether-based TPU, and Polycaprolactone-based TPU. Polyester-based TPU offers good chemical resistance and dynamic properties, making it suitable for applications such as seals and gaskets. Polyether-based TPU is known for its high elasticity and abrasion resistance, making it ideal for components like hoses and tubes. Polycaprolactone-based TPU provides good low-temperature flexibility and toughness, making it suitable for applications where flexibility and durability are crucial, such as automotive interiors.</p></p>
