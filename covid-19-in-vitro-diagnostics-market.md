RDTs provide quick results by detecting viral proteins. ELISAs detect antibodies produced by the body in response to the virus. Neutralization assays measure the ability of antibodies to inhibit viral infection. These different types of tests play a crucial role in diagnosing and monitoring COVID-19, helping healthcare professionals make informed decisions on treatment and prevention strategies.</p></p>
