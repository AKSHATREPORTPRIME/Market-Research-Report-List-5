<p><ul><li>EVA/PE</li><li>PVDC/PE</li><li>PA/PE</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Food shrink bags are commonly categorized into different types based on the material used in their manufacturing. These types include EVA/PE, PVDC/PE, PA/PE, and Others. EVA/PE shrink bags are made from a blend of ethylene vinyl acetate and polyethylene, providing good clarity and flexibility. PVDC/PE bags utilize polyvinylidene chloride to enhance barrier properties against oxygen and moisture. PA/PE bags incorporate polyamide for high puncture resistance and toughness. The "Others" category typically includes unique material blends or specialty coatings tailored to specific food packaging needs.</p></p>
