<p><ul><li>Hydrophilic Polycarbonate Membrane</li><li>Hydrophobic Polycarbonate Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polycarbonate membranes are classified into hydrophilic and hydrophobic types based on their surface properties. Hydrophilic polycarbonate membranes have a strong affinity for water, making them ideal for applications such as filtration and separation where water contact is required. On the other hand, hydrophobic polycarbonate membranes repel water, making them suitable for applications where water needs to be prevented from passing through, such as in gas separation or venting applications. Both types of membranes offer unique advantages for various industrial applications.</p></p>
