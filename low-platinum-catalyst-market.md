<p><ul><li>Pt-based Alloy Catalyst</li><li>Pt-based Core Shell Catalysts</li><li>Pt Single Atom Catalyst</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Low platinum catalyst market consists of three major types: Pt-based alloy catalysts, Pt-based core shell catalysts, and Pt single atom catalysts. Pt-based alloy catalysts are composed of platinum and other metals to enhance catalytic performance. Pt-based core shell catalysts have a core of a different metal enclosed with a platinum shell for improved stability and activity. Pt single atom catalysts have platinum atoms dispersed on a support material, leading to high catalytic efficiency. These types of catalysts are utilized in various industrial processes for their superior catalytic properties.</p></p>
