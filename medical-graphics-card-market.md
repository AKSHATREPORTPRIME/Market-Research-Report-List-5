<p><p>The medical graphics card market consists of two main types: single-head graphics cards and multi-head graphics cards. Single-head graphics cards are designed for applications that only require a single display output, such as individual monitors or workstations. On the other hand, multi-head graphics cards are equipped with multiple display outputs, making them suitable for applications that require simultaneous display on multiple monitors or in multi-monitor setups. Both types of graphics cards cater to specific needs within the medical industry, offering flexibility and customization options for various imaging and diagnostic purposes.</p></p>
