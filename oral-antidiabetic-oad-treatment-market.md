<p><p>Oral antidiabetic (OAD) treatment market includes different types of medications used to manage diabetes. Some common types of OADs are sulphonylureas, which help the pancreas produce more insulin; biguanides, which reduce glucose production in the liver; intestinal α-glucosidase inhibitors, which slow down the digestion of carbohydrates in the intestine; and others, such as thiazolidinediones and DPP-4 inhibitors. Each type of medication works in a different way to help regulate blood sugar levels in individuals with diabetes.</p></p>
