<p><p>Uterine contraction monitors are devices used to measure and monitor contractions during pregnancy and labor. There are two main types: internal uterine contraction monitors, which are inserted into the uterus for direct measurement, and external uterine contraction monitors, which are placed on the abdomen to detect contractions. Internal monitors provide more accurate readings but require invasive insertion, while external monitors are non-invasive but may be less precise. Both types play a crucial role in monitoring and managing labor progress.</p></p>
