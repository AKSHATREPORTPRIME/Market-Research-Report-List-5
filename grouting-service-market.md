<p><p>Grouting services encompass various types of techniques tailored to specific needs. Compaction grouting involves injecting a cement-based grout mix into the ground to stabilize soil and fill voids. Polyurethane grouting utilizes a flexible polyurethane foam to lift and support sinking concrete structures. Cellular grouting creates lightweight, strong material for filling large voids or stabilizing loose soil. Other grouting methods include permeation grouting and jet grouting, which are used for different types of ground improvement and foundation strengthening projects.</p></p>
