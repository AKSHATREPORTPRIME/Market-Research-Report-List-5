<p><ul><li>Static Telepathology</li><li>Dynamic Telepathology</li><li>Hybrid Telepathology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital Pathology Systems refers to the technology that allows the electronic management of pathology images. There are three main types of Digital Pathology Systems markets, which are Static Telepathology, Dynamic Telepathology, and Hybrid Telepathology. Static Telepathology involves the capture and transmission of static images for remote diagnosis. Dynamic Telepathology involves real-time remote consultation using live video feeds. Hybrid Telepathology combines elements of both static and dynamic telepathology for a comprehensive approach to remote diagnosis and consultation.</p></p>
