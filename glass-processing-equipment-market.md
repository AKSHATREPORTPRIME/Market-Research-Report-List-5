<p><p>Glass processing equipment market is segmented into five types: tempered, laminated, insulating, coated, and others. Tempered glass processing involves increasing the strength of the glass through a heating and cooling process. Laminated glass processing involves bonding multiple layers of glass together with a plastic interlayer. Insulating glass processing involves creating a space between two glass panes to improve thermal insulation. Coated glass processing involves applying special coatings for enhanced properties. Other types of glass processing equipment include cutting, grinding, drilling, and polishing machines.</p></p>
