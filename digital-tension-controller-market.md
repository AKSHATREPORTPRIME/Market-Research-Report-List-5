<p><p>Digital tension controllers are available in three main types: automatic, semi-automatic, and manual. Automatic tension controllers are fully automated systems that continuously monitor and adjust tension levels based on set parameters. Semi-automatic tension controllers require occasional manual adjustments, while manual tension controllers rely completely on operator input for tension control. Each type caters to different levels of automation and operator involvement, allowing for flexibility in meeting specific tension control requirements in various industrial applications.</p></p>
