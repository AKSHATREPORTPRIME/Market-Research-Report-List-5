<p><p>The laboratory glassware market can be categorized into different types based on their primary functions. Container glassware includes beakers, test tubes, and flasks used for holding and mixing liquids and chemicals. Measurer glassware, such as pipettes and burettes, are used for accurate measurement of liquids. Filter glassware, like funnels and filter flasks, are used for separating solids from liquids. Other types of glassware include petri dishes, condensers, and distillation apparatus used for various laboratory processes. Each type serves a specific purpose in laboratory experiments and research.</p></p>
