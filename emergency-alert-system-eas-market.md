<p><p>Emergency Alert System (EAS) is a vital tool for disseminating urgent information to the public during emergencies. Two common types of EAS markets are Omni Directional and Rotating Directional. Omni Directional systems transmit alerts in all directions, providing wide coverage but potentially leading to signal interference. Rotating Directional systems have the ability to focus their alerts in specific directions, allowing for targeted messaging and minimizing interference. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring public safety and awareness during critical situations.</p></p>
