<p><strong>The Audiometric Screening Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>OAE Screening Equipment</li><li>ABR Screening Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Audiometric screening equipment includes two main types: OAE (Otoacoustic Emission) Screening Equipment and ABR (Auditory Brainstem Response) Screening Equipment. OAE screening equipment measures the hair cell function in the ear by recording sound waves produced in the cochlea, while ABR screening equipment evaluates the auditory nerve and brainstem's response to sounds. Both types of equipment are crucial for early detection of hearing loss in both adults and children, allowing for timely intervention and treatment.</p></p>
