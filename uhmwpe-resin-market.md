<p><p>UHMWPE resin is categorized into three main market types based on the molecular weight of the polymer chains: Low Range, Medium Range, and High Range. Low Range UHMWPE offers lower wear and abrasion resistance but is cost-effective for applications with less demanding performance requirements. Medium Range UHMWPE provides a balance between cost and performance, ideal for a wide range of industrial applications. High Range UHMWPE offers superior mechanical properties and durability, making it suitable for high-performance applications in industries such as aerospace and defense.</p></p>
