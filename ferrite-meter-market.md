<p><strong>The Ferrite Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Area Probe</li><li>Diameter Probe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ferrite meters are available in two main types: Area Probe and Diameter Probe. Area Probe meters measure the entire cross-sectional area of a component, providing a more comprehensive analysis of ferrite content. On the other hand, Diameter Probe meters focus on measuring the diameter of the component, offering a more precise and localized assessment of ferrite levels. Both types of meters are used in industries such as manufacturing and construction to ensure the quality and performance of ferrite-containing materials.</p></p>
