<p><p>Preimplantation Genetic Diagnostics (PGD) is a procedure used in assisted reproductive technology to screen embryos for genetic disorders before implantation. Two main types of PGD are Preimplantation Genetic Screening (PGS) and Preimplantation Genetic Diagnosis (PGD). PGS is used to screen embryos for chromosomal abnormalities, while PGD is used to diagnose specific genetic conditions in embryos. Both types of PGD aim to increase the likelihood of successful pregnancies and healthy babies for couples undergoing in vitro fertilization.</p></p>
