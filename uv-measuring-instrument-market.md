<p><p>UV Measuring Instruments are classified into different types based on the wavelength range they measure. These types include UV-A (315-400 nm), UV-B (280-315 nm), UV-C (230-280 nm), UV-V (395-445 nm), and Others. UV-A instruments measure long-wave ultraviolet radiation, UV-B instruments measure medium-wave ultraviolet radiation, and UV-C instruments measure short-wave ultraviolet radiation. UV-V instruments measure violet light close to the UV spectrum. The category of Others includes instruments that measure ultraviolet radiation outside of the specified ranges.</p></p>
