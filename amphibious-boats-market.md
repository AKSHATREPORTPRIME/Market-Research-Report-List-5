<p><p>Amphibious boats market can be categorized into different types based on their propulsion systems. Screw propeller propulsion uses rotating blades to move the boat through water. Water jet propulsion utilizes high-pressure water to propel the boat forward. Track-based propulsion involves tracks or treads on the boat for movement on land. Other types of propulsion may include air cushion or hybrid systems. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of efficiency, maneuverability, and versatility for amphibious operations.</p></p>
