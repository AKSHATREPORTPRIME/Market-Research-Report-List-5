<p><strong>The Automotive Engine Chain Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Roller Chain</li><li>Silent Chain</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive engine chains are available in two main types: roller chain and silent chain. Roller chains use rotating rollers between the link plates to reduce friction and noise, making them suitable for high-speed engines. Silent chains use flexible and interlocking links to provide smooth and quiet operation, making them ideal for applications where noise reduction is a priority. Both types of chains play a crucial role in transmitting power from the engine to various components in the vehicle.</p></p>
