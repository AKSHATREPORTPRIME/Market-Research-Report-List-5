<p><p>Negative photoresist chemicals are used in various applications in the semiconductor and electronics industry. Thinner chemicals are used to adjust the viscosity of the photoresist for better coating and adhesion. Flushing agents are used to clean and remove excess photoresist from sensitive areas. Strippers are used to dissolve and remove hardened photoresist after the etching process. Other markets for negative photoresist chemicals include developers, anti-reflective coatings, and additives for enhancing performance. These chemicals play a crucial role in the manufacturing of advanced electronic devices.</p></p>
