<p><p>Air Admittance Valves are classified into three main market types based on pressure conditions: Zero Pressure, Positive Pressure, and Negative Pressure. Zero Pressure valves are used in systems with neutral pressure, Positive Pressure valves are suitable for systems with high pressure, and Negative Pressure valves are designed for low-pressure systems. Each type is essential for maintaining proper air balance and preventing issues such as backflow and siphoning. Selecting the correct valve type is crucial for effective and efficient plumbing systems.</p></p>
