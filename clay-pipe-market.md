<p><p>The clay pipe market can be categorized into two main types: glazed and unglazed pipes. Glazed pipes have a smooth, shiny finish on both the interior and exterior, which makes them more resistant to corrosion and easier to clean. Unglazed pipes, on the other hand, have a rough, porous surface that allows for more water absorption and better adhesion for mortar and sealants.
