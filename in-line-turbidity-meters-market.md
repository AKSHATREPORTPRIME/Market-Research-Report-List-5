<p><p>In-line turbidity meters are used to measure the clarity of liquids by analyzing the amount of scattered light. Optical meters use a light source and a photodetector to measure the intensity of scattered light, while laser meters use a laser light source for more precise measurements. Optical meters are commonly used in industries such as water treatment and food production, while laser meters are preferred in applications requiring higher accuracy, such as pharmaceutical manufacturing. Both types of meters provide real-time data for quality control and process optimization.</p></p>
