<p><ul><li>Static-volume Display</li><li>Swept-volume Display</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Volumetric Display Devices Market is divided into two main types: Static-volume Display and Swept-volume Display. Static-volume Display creates a three-dimensional image by illuminating a fixed volume of space, allowing viewers to see the image from different angles. Swept-volume Display, on the other hand, uses a rapidly moving surface to create the illusion of a three-dimensional image. Both types of displays are used in various applications such as medical imaging, entertainment, and virtual reality to provide realistic and immersive experiences for users.</p></p>
